Marketing Analytics Lecture Notes

These notes accompany MKT 4320 Marketing Analytics and are designed to support in-class lectures, demonstrations, and discussion using R and RStudio.

The focus of these notes is on understanding why we use particular analytical tools and how to interpret results, rather than on memorizing syntax.
